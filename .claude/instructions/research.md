# Research Sources

Trusted sources for researching classical music content for Risør Chamber Music Festival.

---

## Source Types & What They're Good For

### Independent Sources

No commercial relationship with artist:

| Source Type | Good for | Not good for |
|-------------|----------|--------------|
| Newspapers of record | Quotes ABOUT artist, context, critical perspective | - |
| Critics/reviews | Quotes ABOUT artist, artistic evaluation | - |
| Wikipedia | General facts: birth, personal life, deep artistic insight | CV-related content |
| Encyclopedias (Grove, etc.) | Background, historical context | Current activity |

### Artist-Affiliated Sources

Vested interest in presenting artist positively:

| Source Type | Good for | Not good for |
|-------------|----------|--------------|
| Artist website | CV content: collaborations, prizes, discography | Objective perspective |
| Agency/management | CV content, booking info, official bio | Objective perspective |
| Record labels | Discography, recordings, press releases | Objective perspective |
| Interviews & features | Quotes BY the artist | Quotes ABOUT the artist |

---

## How to Use Both Source Types

Both source types provide essential information:

| Source Type | Role in Content |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Independent** | More trustworthy for marketing/communication text. Should be highlighted. Forms the core of persuasive content. |
| **Artist-affiliated** | Essential for CV content (collaborations, prizes, discography). Acts as "spice" that confirms and supplements the independent sources. |

**In practice:** Lead with independent sources for credibility, support with affiliated sources for factual CV details.

### Scores & Works

| Source | URL | Use for |
|--------|-----|---------|
| IMSLP | imslp.org | Sheet music, work catalogs, opus numbers |

**Work catalog conventions:**
- BWV (Bach-Werke-Verzeichnis) - Bach
- KV/K (Köchel-Verzeichnis) - Mozart
- Op. (Opus) - General numbering
- D (Deutsch catalog) - Schubert
- Hob. (Hoboken catalog) - Haydn

**Technical music details:**
- Movement names and tempo markings
- Key signatures (see `translation-rules.md` for NO↔EN conventions)
- Instrumentation and scoring

## Desirable Content Types

What to look for when researching:

| Content Type | Examples |
|--------------|----------|
| Biographical facts | Birth, training, teachers, career milestones |
| Achievements | Awards, competitions, notable premieres |
| Artistic identity | Repertoire focus, style, regular collaborators |
| Connection to Norway/Risør | Previous performances, Norwegian repertoire, collaborations |
| Recent activity | New albums, tours, projects, upcoming events |
